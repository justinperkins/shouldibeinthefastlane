Should you be driving in the fast lane?

Use the questions below to help you figure out what to do.

  • Are you passing somebody? Yes, now step on it.
  • Is there a car behind you? No, move over.
  • Are you driving under the speed limit? No, move over.
  • Are you driving the speed limit? No, move over.
  • Are you talking on the phone? No, move over.
  • Are you doing your makeup? No, move over.
